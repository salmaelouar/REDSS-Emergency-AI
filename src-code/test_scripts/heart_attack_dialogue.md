# Emergency Call Dialogue: Heart Attack

## English Version
**Dispatcher:** 911, what's your emergency?

**Caller:** [Man, voice tight with panic] My dad is clutching his chest! He says he can't breathe and his left arm is numb!

**Dispatcher:** What's your address, sir?

**Caller:** 3847 Riverside Boulevard!

**Dispatcher:** How old is your father?

**Caller:** He's 62! He's sweating really bad and he looks gray!

**Dispatcher:** Is he conscious right now?

**Caller:** Yes, but he's doubled over! He says the pain is like an elephant sitting on his chest!

**Dispatcher:** Does he have any heart problems or take heart medication?

**Caller:** Yes! He had a stent put in two years ago! He takes blood pressure pills!

**Dispatcher:** What's his name?

**Caller:** Robert Martinez!

**Dispatcher:** Is he sitting or lying down?

**Caller:** He's sitting on the couch!

**Dispatcher:** Good. Keep him sitting up. Don't let him lie flat. Is he breathing good?

**Caller:** [Panicked] Really fast and shallow! He keeps saying he's going to die!

**Dispatcher:** The paramedics are 3 minutes away. Stay on the line with me. Is the pain getting worse?

**Caller:** [Dad groaning in background] He's nodding yes! Oh God, please hurry!
